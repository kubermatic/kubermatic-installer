Helm values.yaml

Make sure to download the values.yaml for Helm in case you want to further modify and re-deploy the charts in the future, but since it contains your kubeconfig and other credentials, keep it secret.

Installer Manifest

We also recommend that you keep a copy of your configuration manifest that you've created with this wizard. This allows you to re-run the installer at a later point in time without having to re-enter all information again. As with the values.yaml, your manifest also contains private information and should be treated carefully.
